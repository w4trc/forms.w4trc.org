About the Event

W4TRC will be operating as W1AW/4 Portable from Warriors Path State Park as part of the ARRL America250 Worked All States event — a year-long operating event celebrating the 250th anniversary (Semiquincentennial) of the signing of the Declaration of Independence.

All contacts will be uploaded to Logbook of The World (LoTW). There are no paper QSL cards for W1AW/Portable stations during this event. Activity is encouraged on all FCC-authorized bands (excluding 60 meters) on CW, Phone, and Digital.

The America250-WAS award is earned by contacting all 50 states in 2026, with endorsements available for:

  • Contacting W1AW/Portable stations in all 50 states
  • Contacting ARRL Affiliated Club call signs in all 50 states
  • Triple Play — all 50 states on CW, Phone, and Digital
