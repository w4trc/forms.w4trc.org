About Field Day

ARRL Field Day is ham radio's largest on-the-air event, held annually on the fourth full weekend of June. W4TRC will be setting up and operating as a club — come join us for food, fellowship, and radio!

We're also making it a potluck, so please let us know what you plan to bring so we can coordinate and make sure we have a great spread. Fill out the form below to let us know you're coming.
